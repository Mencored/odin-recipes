Taco Recipe

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make taco recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chopped onion

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing the seasoned beef in the center of each tortilla, then top with shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, and chopped onions.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
