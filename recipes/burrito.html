Burrito Recipe

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make burrito recipe that's perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup seasoned ground beef
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package directions.
  2. Season the ground beef with your favorite spices and cook until browned.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet.
  4. Assemble the burritos by placing the rice, beef, cheese, tomatoes, and onions in the center of each tortilla.
  5. Scoop a dollop of sour cream onto each burrito.
  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll it up tightly.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
